GroomSquad

Role

UX Design

Agency

Personal

Year

2015

GroomSquad is a service that has hair stylists come to you (or your group) for your grooming needs. The main idea was to be a "no frills" type of site that gave the clients and potential clients the information they needed quickly. I designed the site to be a clean, one page site that is easily navigated and can be adapted to a responsive design. The business went on hiatus and so did the site design.
